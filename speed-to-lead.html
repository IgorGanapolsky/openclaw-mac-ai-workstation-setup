Common questions

Will my customers know they're talking to an AI?

Eventually yes — when they engage substantively, the agent transparently flags itself and offers a human handoff. The goal isn't to deceive; it's to make sure they get an instant, useful response instead of nothing for nine minutes.

What if it books someone who shouldn't have been booked?

The qualification rules are tuned during the pilot. The agent escalates ambiguous cases to you by text before booking. In the rare case of a bad booking, the recovered-revenue report flags it and we tighten the rules.

I already use Mindbody / Jane / Squarespace. Do I have to migrate?

No. The agent integrates with what you have. We have not yet found a mainstream small-business scheduler we can't connect to.

# SOP: Value-Based Offer & Copywriting (Hormozi/StoryBrand)
*Framework: $100M Offers & Building a StoryBrand*

## 1. The Value Equation
When drafting any product description or offer, maximize these four variables:
- **Dream Outcome:** Quantify the "End State" (e.g., "$10k/mo revenue", "0 missed leads").
- **Perceived Likelihood:** Use social proof (testimonials) and a "Risk-Reversal" guarantee.
- **Time Delay:** How fast do they get results? (e.g., "Same-day setup", "1-hour diagnostic").
- **Effort & Sacrifice:** How much work do they do? (e.g., "Done-for-you", "Zero configuration").

## 2. The StoryBrand Message (SB7)
Position the **Customer as the Hero**, and **You as the Guide**.
1. **Character:** Local business owner / AI enthusiast.
2. **Problem:** Missed leads (External), frustration/overwhelm (Internal), unfair loss of market share (Philosophical).
3. **Guide:** Empathy (I've been there) + Authority (Ex-Subway Lead / ThumbGate Creator).
4. **Plan:** Clear 3-step path (1. Audit, 2. Deploy, 3. Recover).
5. **CTA:** Direct "Buy Now" or "Book Audit."
6. **Failure:** Loss of revenue, tech complexity.
7. **Success:** Passive lead recovery, time freedom.

## 3. Implementation Task
- **Action:** Audit every Gumroad and Outreach description against the Value Equation. 
- **Checklist:** Does this offer reduce effort to zero? Does it have a specific guarantee?
